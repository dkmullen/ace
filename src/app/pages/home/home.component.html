The ACE Awards at the Clarence Brown Theatre
A theatre contest open to high school students from Knox and surrounding counties
Sunday January 9, 2022
Alumni Memorial Building (AMB) Room 209 (map)
Performance times will be by appointment. This is a non-audience event.
Awards in Five Categories
Best Contemporary Comedic Monologue
Best Contemporary Dramatic Monologue
Best Shakespeare or Classical Monologue
Best Musical Theatre Song
Best Overall Acting Award*
*A student can win no more than one of the five awards. However, students can enter multiple categories and the contest is free.
Awards are $500 to the winning student and $200 to their high school theatre program.
Monologues and songs must be under 2 minutes in length. Each participant gets 6 minutes to perform and respond to an adjustment. This is a non-audience event. All participants work with the four adjudicators only in the room.
