Details

All participating students must be of high school grade level or below to participate (college contests coming soon).

If you need more information, please contact jay@aceknox.com.

We ask that all performances are newly recorded and not from a recorded stage version of a previous performance.

Musical Theatre - all songs must be from previously produced material.

Monologues - we would prefer modern monologues (we will be holding a Shakespeare contest later in the school year). We would also prefer that your monologue comes from a play and not a free-standing piece.

*If you need help finding a monologue or musical theatre song, or if you are interested in coaching for your piece, ACE has an available team of professional coaches. Please contact jay@aceknox.com for guidance.

*The judging will take place from a national panel of judges representing Broadway houses, theatre companies, undergraduate and graduate theatre programs, and professional acting coaches unaffiliated with ACE. We guarantee you will get an honest and fair judging of your work.

For any questions, please contact jay@aceknox.com.

Thank you and best of luck.
