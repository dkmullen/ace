ACE Singing Awards
JANUARY 11, 2020 - BIJOU THEATRE

Registration is CLOSED

The Alliance for Creative Excellence (ACE) is hosting the 8th Annual ACE Singing Awards for Knoxville area students. Callbacks will be in mid December. The awards show and contest will take place at the historic Bijou Theatre in downtown Knoxville on January 11, 2020 at 7pm. Participants will compete for prizes for themselves and for their school (or, for home-schoolers, a charity of their choice).

    Categories:
  • Rising Star: Grades 8 and below
  • Individual Vocal: Grades 9-12; can be accompanied
  • Individual Instrumental: Grades 9-12; must accompany yourself
  • Choir/A Capella: Groups of 4+, Grades 6-12

Contact jay@aceknox.com if you have questions.
