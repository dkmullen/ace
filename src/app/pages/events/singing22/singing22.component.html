A singing contest for middle and high school students from Knox and surrounding counties
TWO AWARDS
Best Open Singing - High School
Best Rising Star - Middle School
Prize - $500 winner, $250 to the school of the winner
Audience Choice - $200 Gift Card
Please submit a one-minute video of your song - any song, any genre. You may sing to a track, sing a capella, be accompanied, or accompany yourself. We prefer your submission to be on YouTube with a link, but you can also share a video from your phone.
IMPORTANT DATES
First Round Submission: April 13 - 29
Finalists Announcement (3 per category) on April 30
Final Round - May 1 - 7 - Finalists will have videos posted online for judges and audience voting.
May 8 - 15 - Submissions of a full song for each finalist
May 15 - Winners announced
