ACE Safer@Home Photography Contest

For students through grade 12 in Knox County, Tennessee and surrounding counties.

The theme: How has being out of school changed your life the most? (No words. Just a picture).
Results: Thanks to everyone who entered this contest. Each of these five finalists did a nice job capturing an idea. We have an Overall Winner (selected by judges) and an Audience Choice winner (selected by votes). In addition, we have picked two photographers for Honorable Mention. Congratulations and thank you!

    Judges' comments:
  • "I like this photo for its color (the beautiful sky). I also love the perspective, looking down on the parking lot from on high, but through a fence and slightly askew. All of this could be suggesting the loneliness and disorientation of quarantine, separation from friends and fun. I don't love the use of portrait (vertical) orientation, and yet maybe it adds to the theme here, like looking at the big world through a narrow window."
