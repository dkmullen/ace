ACE Safer@Home Photography Contest

For students through grade 12 in Knox County, Tennessee and surrounding counties.

Photo by kazuend on Unsplash
Here's your theme:
How has being out of school changed your life the most? No words. Just a picture.
Take your picture and send it, along with your name and the name of your school to jay@aceknox.com. We will display it here at aceknox.com, where people can vote for it as their favorite. Judges will take a look at it too.
There will be two prizes awarded:
  • Judges' Prize: $150
  • Audience Votes: $100
Photos are due by 9:00pm on Saturday, April 18, 2020. Audience voting will run from Sunday, April 19 at Noon through Friday, April 24 at 9:00pm.
*Please observe all current recommendations about social distancing. This is designed to be an individual contest which does not require a gathering of people.
