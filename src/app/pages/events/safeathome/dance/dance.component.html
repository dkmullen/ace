ACE Safer@Home Dance Contest

For students through grade 12 in Knox County, Tennessee and surrounding counties.

Photo by Marc A. Sporys on Unsplash
You think you can dance? Make a 90-second (or less) video of yourself. Any style, any music.
Post your video to YouTube, and send the link, your name and the name of your school to jay@aceknox.com. We will embed it here at aceknox.com, where people can vote for it as their favorite. Judges will take a look at it too.
There will be two prizes awarded:
  • Judges' Prize: $150
  • Audience Votes: $100
Videos are due by 9:00pm on Saturday, May 2, 2020. Audience voting will run from Sunday, May 3 at Noon through Friday, May 8 at 9:00pm.
*Please observe all current recommendations about social distancing. This is designed to be an individual contest which does not require a gathering of people.
