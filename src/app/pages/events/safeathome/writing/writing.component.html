ACE Safer@Home Creative Writing Contest

For students through grade 12 in Knox County, Tennessee and surrounding counties.

Photo by Alexis Brown on Unsplash
Write a 1000 word (or less) essay on this topic:
We mostly like music written in and for our particular generation. Write an essay that convinces others not in your generation that your music is worth a listen.
Send your essay, your name and the name of your school to jay@aceknox.com. We will post it here at aceknox.com. There won't be online voting for this contest, but our judges will choose two winners:
  • First Prize: $150
  • Second Prize: $100
Essays are due by 9:00pm on Saturday, April 25, 2020.
*Please observe all current recommendations about social distancing. This is designed to be an individual contest which does not require a gathering of people.
