ACE Safer@Home Shakespeare Contest

For students through grade 12 in Knox County, Tennessee and surrounding counties.

Happy Birthday William Shakespeare!
To celebrate William Shakespeare's Birthday on April 23rd, ACE will hold a Safer@home Shakespeare Monologue Contest. Below are four monologues to choose from. Pick one. Video yourself performing it. Post the video to YouTube, then fill out the form below. Deadline is April 23rd.

Choose one of these four monologues (click the links below to see the monologues):

  • Emilia - Othello (start with "But I do think)
  • Portia - Merchant of Venice
  • Hamlet - Hamlet
  • Benedick - Much Ado About Nothing
We will post your videos on our website and judges will select the top two monologues. (No audience voting for this contest). Each winner will receive a $100 gift card. Contact jay@aceknox.com for any questions.
Videos are due by 9:00pm on Thursday, April 23, 2020. Winners will be announced shortly after that.
