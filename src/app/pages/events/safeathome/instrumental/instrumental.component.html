ACE Safer@Home Instrumental Contest

For students through grade 12 in Knox County, Tennessee and surrounding counties.

Photo by Fitsum Admasu on Unsplash
Do you play an instrument? Any instrument? Make a video of yourself playing your best song (three minutes or less), post it to YouTube, and fill out the form below. We will embed your video here at aceknox.com, where people can vote for it as their favorite. Judges will take a look at it too. Questions? Contact jay@aceknox.com.
There will be two prizes awarded:
  • Judges' Prize: $150
  • Audience Votes: $100
Videos are due by 9:00pm on Saturday, April 11, 2020. Audience voting will run from Sunday, April 12 at Noon through Friday, April 17 at 9:00pm.
