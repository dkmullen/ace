ACE Acting Awards
ONE DAY ACTING EVENT: JANUARY 25, 2020

Have a chance to win $500 for you and another $250 for your school!

One TEAM will win $1000!

    Categories:
  • Dramatic Monologue
  • Comedic Monologue
  • Shakespeare Monologue
  • Musical Theatre Song
  • *NEW* - TEAM Category
Individual Competition:

All individuals can compete in any and all categories. To audition, you must have a monologue or song that qualifies for the category.

Four (4) Finalists will be picked to perform in each monologue category.

We may have more finalists for the Musical Theatre Category.

Team Competition:

Any school may enter the TEAM competition by putting together 4 individuals for each category to compete as a team. These individuals will automatically be entered into the individual categories as well. A school may have as many teams as they would like, but an individual cannot perform in the same team category twice.*

Three teams will be picked as finalists.

Auditions:

Auditions will take place the morning and afternoon of January 25th on UT Campus (TBA)

All competitors will come to the show (see below) where the finalists will be announced and perform live that night.

Show (finals):

January 25th, 2020, 7pm at Old City Performing Arts Center

*4 individuals could form 4 different teams, but they must each perform a different category for each team. For example - a musical theatre performer would only be allowed to be on one team. If he/she entered on another team, it would have to be under a different category. An individual competing for a TEAM is welcome to audition for any and all other categories as an individual, but he/she may only perform on one TEAM per category.
