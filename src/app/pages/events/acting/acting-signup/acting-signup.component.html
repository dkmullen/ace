Team competition

Any school may enter the TEAM competition by putting together 4 individuals for each category to compete as a team. These individuals will automatically be entered into the individual categories as well. A school may have as many teams as they would like, but an individual cannot perform in the same team category twice. Three teams will be picked as finalists..
