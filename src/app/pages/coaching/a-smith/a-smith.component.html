Anna Catherine Smith is a recent graduate of the BFA Musical Theatre program at Pace University. Favorite credits include: Belle in Beauty and the Beast, Ariel in The Little Mermaid (Timber Lake Playhouse), Philoclea in Head Over Heels (Pace University), and Hope Cladwell in Urinetown (Oak Ridge Playhouse). In addition to theater, Anna has been seen numerous times on Saturday Night Live (NBC) and Snapped: Killer Couples (Oxygen).

When Anna is not performing, she is coaching middle and high school theater students who have the passion and drive to follow their dreams. Anna's previous coaching experience includes working with the ACE/Bijou Awards at the Bijou Theater with Jay Apking.
