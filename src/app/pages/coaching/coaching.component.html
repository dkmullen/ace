ACE COACHES & MENTORS

The various teachers you see on this page have a wide background in performance education and expertise. We have combined to perform in almost every state and in multiple countries, and based on our knowledge, we now join forces to develop a teaching formula that works perfectly for any audition including monologues, songs or readings.

First and foremost, we will help you pick the pieces that are right for YOU, or help you with the specific pieces you have been given to prepare.

We then delve into our formula which include the understanding, the focus, the objective, and the tactics of YOU in the piece.

We then add the layers of character including the who, where, when and how that will make your piece stand out.

Finally, we turn our attention on the rules of entering an audition, auditioning, and leaving the audition.

This formula, which has come from decades of auditioning, directing and coaching, will give you the complete preparation you need to unleash your talent in an audition.

We have seen and made many mistakes in our careers. We know what directors and colleges want to see. Let us help you perfect this important skill of auditioning. Click on any coach to learn more.
