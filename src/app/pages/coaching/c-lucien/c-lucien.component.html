Courtney Lucien is based in Cincinnati, OH where she is in her sixth season as a resident ensemble member with the Cincinnati Shakespeare Company/ She graduated from Indiana University with her BA in Theatre and Drama, and has since then been performing around the Midwest. Some favorite roles include Anne Frank in The Diary of Anne Frank, Emma in Emma, Desdemona in Othello, as well as Daisy Buchanan in The Great Gatsby (Cardinal Stage Company).

Courtney has experience teaching students in private lessons, Shakespeare summer camp, the semester-long Shakespeare "Groundlings" program with Cincinnati Shakespeare Company, and many school workshops. She specializes in Shakespeare, but has plenty of experience with contemporary theatre as well as musical theatre.

Courtney loves helping students feel comfortable and confident in their work. She believes that being your authentic self is the most valuable asset you can bring into an audition room.
