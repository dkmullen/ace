Matt Lytle is an actor and teacher currently residing in NYC. He holds his BFA in Theatre Arts from Boston University. While pursuing his BFA, he spent a semester studying Shakespeare and Restoration Acting at the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art. He holds his MFA in Acting from Brown University. As an actor, he has performed for regional theatre companies in Louisville, Cincinnati, Nashville, St. Louis, Providence, Saratoga Springs and recently played Romeo in Romeo & Juliet at the Alabama Shakespeare Festival. He has a particular love of Shakespeare and has performed in 19 professional Shakespeare productions.

As a teacher, he has lead workshops/camps across the country, taught an undergraduate acting course at Brown University and is a private acting coach in NYC.
