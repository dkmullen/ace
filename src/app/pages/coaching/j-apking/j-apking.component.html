Jay Apking is the creator and artistic director of ACE. He is a professional actor, director and teacher currently residing in Knoxville, Tennessee.

As an actor, Jay has performed for companies in Chicago, Cincinnati and Virginia's Barter Theatre, including a tour that took him to Russia.

Favorite roles include Hamlet, Benedick in Much Ado About Nothing, Mark Antony in Julius Caesar, and Wiley in Wiley and The Hairy Man.

He has directed dozens of plays from Shakespeare's Romeo & Juliet to Mamet's Oleanna, and has been teaching and coaching theatre in various states for over 25 years.

Jay created The Janus Project in Cincinnati - a theatre dedicated to education and performance in the arts.
