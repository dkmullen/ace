The Alliance for Creative Excellence (ACE) is a non-profit organization established to promote the creative arts to students, families and teachers in and around the Knoxville area. ACE creates opportunities and incentives for performance and education through classes, workshops, camps and award shows in many areas of the creative arts. It is our goal to give all young people the opportunity to thrive through the creative arts.
